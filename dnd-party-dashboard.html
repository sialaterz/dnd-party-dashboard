Ambush Party Cheat Sheet

A tactical webpage for Eilrys, Zo, Kenshi, Evangeline, and Lixie — built around scouting, ambushes, control, mobility, and quick decision-making at the table.

Party identity: mobile tactical skirmishers

Big strength: ambush + exploration

Big weakness: no true tank

What to Study First

Do not study the whole rulebook first. Study the rules that affect your character’s decisions every session.

↪ Every Turn

Ask: What is my Action? Bonus Action? Movement? Reaction? Can I use cover? Can enemies reach me?

♡ Emergency Buttons

Healing Word picks allies up at range. Cure Wounds works in touch range. Thunderwave creates space. Fog Cloud can cover retreat.

☠ Kill Order

Usually target enemy casters first, then archers, then fast enemies, then heavy melee enemies. Focus fire instead of spreading damage.
